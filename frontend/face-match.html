📖 How similarity is calculated: Face descriptors (128-point embeddings) are extracted from both images using Microsoft's ResNet-based face recognition model. The Euclidean distance between the two descriptors is converted to a similarity percentage. A score above 75% indicates a strong match. Between 55–75% is inconclusive (lighting or angle may differ). Below 55% suggests different individuals.

🔒 Privacy: All face processing happens exclusively in your browser using TensorFlow.js. No face image or biometric data is ever uploaded to any server.
